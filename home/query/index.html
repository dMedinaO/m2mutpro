PESB2 Group information

PESB2 group, is an interdisciplinary group, which focuses its research on protein engineering and the application of bioinformatics techniques to the study of complex systems. It is directed by PhD Álvaro Olivera Nappa and is made up of post doc students, doctorate and magister students, as well as undergraduate students who do research work or their thesis. The center's research is based on both laboratory aspects and the development of mathematical models for complex systems using differential modeling or data mining techniques. Several tools have been developed focused mainly on the analysis of patterns and classification / regression models. For more information, visit the website of PESB2
